Privacy Policy for Minion Management

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Crowsoda Gaming ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Minion Management (the "Game"). By playing the Game, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, Facebook, or email, we receive a basic account identifier and, where you provide it, a display name and email address. We use this to create and restore your game profile.
  • Game data. We store your progress, currencies, roster, purchases, and settings so they persist across sessions and devices.
  • Advertising identifier. Our advertising provider (Google AdMob) uses your device's advertising ID to serve rewarded ads and limit ad fraud.
  • Device and diagnostic data. We may collect basic device and crash information to keep the Game running and to diagnose problems.

How we use information

  • To provide, save, and synchronize your game progress.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To show rewarded advertisements and grant the associated in-game rewards.
  • To operate leaderboards and player-versus-player features.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues.

Service providers

We use trusted third-party services that process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, and hosting) — Firebase Privacy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Login (sign-in) — Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services (distribution and in-app purchases) — Google Privacy Policy

Children

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Advertising is configured as not child-directed with a maximum content rating suitable for teen audiences.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your account and game data for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the Game: open Settings and choose Delete Account. This removes your profile and game data from our systems. If you would rather not use the in-game control, you can also request deletion by contacting us at the email below. We honor deletion requests within a reasonable period, except where retention is required to comply with legal obligations or prevent fraud.

Your choices

  • You can reset or opt out of ad personalization through your device's advertising settings.
  • You can delete your account and data in-game via Settings > Delete Account, or request access or deletion by contacting us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: crowsoda@crowsodagaming.com
